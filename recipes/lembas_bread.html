Lembas Bread

Elven Lembas Bread. One small bite is enough to fill the stomach of a grown man!

Lembas was made first by Yavanna from special corn that grew in Aman, and OromÃ« gave it to the Elves of the Great Journey.

Only on rare occasions was it given to non-Elves, because it was believed that mortals who ate it would become wary of their mortality and would desire to live among the Elves. Let us hope a similar fate does not befall the souls who follow this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 C Honey
  • 1 Tbsp Grated orange peel -or- 3 Kumquats or 1 large finger of a hand of Buddha
  • 2 Tbsp Orange flower water optional
  • 3 oz Blanched almonds
  • 1/4 C Melted butter
  • 2 & 1/4 C Semolina flour
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Instructions

  1. Place eggs, honey, orange peel (or choice of fruit). orange flower water, and almonds in blender. Blend on high for 3 minutes.
  2. Add 1 cup of the flour. Blend for 1 minute.
  3. Empty blender into a bowl and add remaining flour and salt. Whisk until well-blended.
  4. Spoon lembas onto a pre-heated pizzelle or krumkake iron for 15 seconds on each side (or until lightly browned)
Note (if substituting waffle iron):
  • Add 1 tsp Baking powder
  • Texture may be slightly altered
